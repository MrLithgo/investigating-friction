Investigating Friction

0 10 20 30

Instructions:

  1. Select a surface type from the control panel
  2. Add masses to increase the weight of the block
  3. Click and drag the newtonmeter to pull the block
  4. Notice how the force spikes initially (static friction) then drops (kinetic friction)
  5. The kinetic friction force will be displayed in the control panel
  6. Record your observations in the data table
  7. Repeat three times for each surface and take an average
